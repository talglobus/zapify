Zapify

A better, faster way of putting things in your hands
while keeping your phone and wallet in your pocket

When you're you, the transaction goes through

Our mission is to make shopping more efficient through a mobile phone payment system we call Zapify. What makes us unique is that our system allows you to pay without having to take out anything.

Our vision

A new evolution in financial convenience. Using bleeding edge facial recognition and machine learning algorithms to authenticate users and render fraud effectively impossible. Zapify will change the way you spend.
